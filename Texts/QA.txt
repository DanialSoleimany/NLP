The Eiffel Tower is a famous landmark located in Paris, France. It was completed in 1889 and has become one of the most iconic structures in the world. The tower stands at a height of 324 meters and was initially constructed as the entrance arch for the 1889 World's Fair, which was held in Paris to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the French Revolution.
